## 🤖 What is the Agentic AI Bootcamp?

The **Agentic AI Bootcamp** is a hands-on, modular learning experience designed by **IBM Client Engineering** for Clients and Business Partners. The goal is for the Client or Business Partner team to build an Agentic AI application to address an enterprise use case in 1-2 days. 

### 🚀 Customize it!
🔨 **Build your own bootcamp** curriculum: pick your learning materials, hands-on labs, demos, and other resources, and tailor the bootcamp to your Client or Business Partner, depending on their interests and skill levels.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> This repo contains instructions and materials to help you **customize** and run your own Agentic AI bootcamp for clients. However, you're welcome to bring your own resources as well!
> Since each client has different skill levels, expectations, and interests, it's essential to tailor the material to their needs before the bootcamp.


## 🎯 What's the goal?
🚀 Show the value of IBM's Agentic AI technology quickly with lower commitment from the client.

🚀 Get clients excited with a tangible outcome and learning experience, generate a pilot, or progress a deal. 

🚀 **Audience:** Clients or Business Partners interested in leveraging Agentic AI.

🚀 **Run by:** AI Engineers, BTLs, Designers from CE, Brand Technical Specialists, CSMs (as applicable), and more!

## 🎨 5. Study the murals

[Mural templates](https://app.mural.co/template/e193e58d-71f9-4955-b70a-b0486834d442/fa3056a9-b0ce-45a3-bbf1-dbaa12fc0d0c) have been built specifically for Agentic AI. They might be used in two scenarios:

1. The client brings their own use case. In this case, the murals can guide the use case discovery session. For this scenario, it is very important to make sure that the scope of the use case is able to be contained within the 2 days of the bootcamp.

2. After the bootcamp, the client is interested in discussing a use case for a pilot. In this case, the scope of the use case can be broader, but similarly, it is important to make sure it can be contained within the 2-4 week scope of the pilot.

## 🏆 5. Tell participants about the badge!
After successful completion of the bootcamp, participants can claim an official badge. To do so, they should go to a webpage where they register and claim the badge. We will ensure that only participants who were at an actual bootcamp will be granted one.

[This is the page](https://ibm.box.com/s/v1bazhq5cx7sjhwyxjwfm61c67o2i4hd) that you can show to attendants in your final wrapup sessions. It has a QR code they can scan to find the webpage. We recommend not showing this QR code until the end so that they cannot claim the badge if they leave the bootcamp halfway.
The page says the QR code is only good for 7 days, but that is not enforced, it is only there to encourage quick follow up.

Note that the badge is only available to customers, not IBMers.
